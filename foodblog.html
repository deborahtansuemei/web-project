LONDON'S TOP PANCAKES

Everything you need to know about London's best pancakes from a pancake enthusiast

TOP PANCAKES

Sunday Cafe

Buttermilk pancakes with bacon and maple syrup is the way to go at Sundays. You'll have to queue for this but rest assured, it is worth it!

The Locals Cafe

Matcha Pancakes with a blueberry compote and white chocolate shavings. Matcha fans, this ones for you!

;

Darwin Brasserie

Pancakes with a view? Darwin is the place to be! Nutella and banana pancakes, One for the chocolate fanatics!

Dalloway Terrace

Buttermilk Pancakes with blueberries and lemon curd. Be sure to snap a pic of the flower wall!

Chiltern Firehouse

Buttermilk pancakes with blueberries and a serving of yoghurt and a generous drizzle of maple syrup. So fluffy and sure to fill you up.

;

Granger & Co

HANDS DOWN Best ricotta hotcakes lathered with honeycomb butter, dusted with sugar and topped with banana slices. Pure heaven!

Scarlett Green

Buttermilk blueberry pancakes with fresh berries, mascarpone and pure maple syrup! Whether you are dining here or in the alternative 7 branches across London, these pancakes should be a go to!

The Wolseley

Fat, fluffy pancakes stacked up and lathered in coulis with plump little berries. Soak yourself in an institution filled with a constant buzz.

;

Burnt Toast Cafe

Buttermlik pancakes with caramelized bananas and chocolate!. With queues stretching up to 1 hr, you'll have to get there early to get your hands on these!
